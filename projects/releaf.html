Releaf

A team project made for the StarterHacks 2019 hackathon. Releaf is a mobile application and website focused around the user's mental well-being. From chatbots, meditation sessions, mood journaling features, to various external resources, Releaf hopes to be an all-round mental health app for audiences of all ages.
